Local lending is a platform where people can lend their belongings to those who need them. Your items are secured via in-person authentication, so you can have the peace of mind knowing that your lent items are going to be used and returned responsibly.

Features

  • Totally Free to Use

    Local lending is free to use amongst your peers.

  • Easy to Use

    Users of Local lending just have to sign up, search for something that they need, and then meet up.

  • Safe and secure

    All transactions are approved by both parties at the time of exchange to make sure nobody gets scammed.
